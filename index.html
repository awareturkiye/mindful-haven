How to Take Care of Your Mental Health

Simple, evidence-based steps you can take today to improve and maintain your mental wellbeing.

1

Talk to someone you trust about how you're feeling

2

Stay physically active — even a short walk makes a difference

3

Eat well, limit alcohol, and prioritise sleep

4

Practice mindfulness or relaxation techniques daily

5

Seek professional help if things feel unmanageable

Explore Guide
🤝

Supporting Someone Else

If someone you care about is struggling, knowing how to help can make all the difference in their recovery.

1

Listen without judgement — just being present matters

2

Ask directly if they're having thoughts of self-harm

3

Help them access professional support when ready

4

Look after your own wellbeing too — it's not selfish

5

Keep checking in — recovery takes time and patience
